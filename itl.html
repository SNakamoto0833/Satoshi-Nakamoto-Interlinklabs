$ITL — The Human Currency

A currency designed for verified people — for payments, microtasks, humanitarian aid, and fair data compensation.

Design & Purpose

$ITL is created to serve everyday human economic activity within the InterLink Network: simple, low-friction transfers between verified people, with strong sybil resistance and fairness built in.

Core characteristics

  • One person, one account model for distribution
  • Designed for micropayments and real-world inclusion
  • Earnable through tasks, education, and verified contributions

Real use cases

  • Humanitarian aid: direct transfers to verified recipients in crisis zones.
  • Education & health credits: targeted support for essential services.
  • AI data & crowdsourcing: compensating verified contributors for high-quality human data.
  • Peer-to-peer commerce: social marketplaces and micro-economies among verified users.

Interaction With $ITLG & ITLX

$ITL functions alongside $ITLG and ITLX: $ITL for broad transactional use, $ITLG for governance & ecosystem privileges, and ITLX for exchange and cross-chain liquidity.

The DAO defines conversion mechanics, staking rules, and any bridges between $ITL and other tokens to maintain stability and fairness.
