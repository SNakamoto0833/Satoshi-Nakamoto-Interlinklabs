ITLX — Perpetual Exchange, Spot Swap & Bridge

A composable, omnichain exchange suite designed for the Human Network.

Overview

ITLX is an integrated exchange and bridging suite built to deliver deep liquidity, low slippage, and multi-chain access while preserving non-custodial user control.

Core modules

  • Perpetual Engine: permissionless perpetual contracts with USDC settlement and dynamic funding.
  • Spot Swap: fast, aggregated liquidity routing for trustless token swaps.
  • Bridge: secure, optimized cross-chain transfers with verifiable execution.

Why ITLX for InterLink

ITLX is built specifically to serve human-verified markets and applications — ensuring fair access, reduced manipulation, and composability across dApps that require identity assurance.

  • Permissionless liquidity across chains
  • Low-latency execution and liquidity routing
  • Developer SDKs for integrating trading into mini-apps
  • Transparent, auditable settlement and oracle feeds

Security & Pricing

ITLX uses reliable off-chain oracles, on-chain settlement, and risk-management systems (cross-margin, dynamic funding, liquidation protections) to deliver execution quality similar to centralized exchanges — while keeping custody with users.

Integration with InterLink

ITLX integrates with InterLink Wallet and the Human Network to provide verified trading experiences, preferential access for $ITLG holders, and fee redistribution models that reward verified contributors.
