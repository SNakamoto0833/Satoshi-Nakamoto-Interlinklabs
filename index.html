The InterLink DAO places governance power in the hands of verified human $ITLG holders. The DAO implements open proposal, discussion, and voting processes recorded on-chain for full transparency.

Governance mechanics (high-level)

  • Proposal Phase: Community members submit proposals (governance changes, allocations, protocol updates).
  • Discussion & Review: Proposals undergo a review period where stakeholders discuss impacts and adjustments.
  • Voting: Verified $ITLG holders cast votes. Voting weight can reflect contribution and hold-time depending on DAO parameters.
