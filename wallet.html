Redefining Wallets Through Verified Identity

InterLink Wallet is natively built into the InterLink App and anchored to InterLink ID — a privacy-first human verification system. It is a non-custodial wallet that links on-chain utility with human authenticity.

Key principles

  • Privacy-preserving verification: verify humanity without exposing sensitive data.
  • Non-custodial by design: users control private keys and retain sovereignty.
  • Human-first UX: designed to be intuitive for first-time users and powerful for builders.

Multi-chain by Design

InterLink Wallet supports multiple blockchains from the same interface — Ethereum, Polygon, BNB Chain, Solana, and more — enabling frictionless transfers and native dApp access without switching wallets.

Features

  • Cross-chain asset view and simple network switching
  • Integrated mini-app browser for human-gated dApps
  • Secure key storage and recovery flow tailored for real humans

Gateway to Human-Verified dApps

With InterLink Wallet + InterLink ID you get a single identity that unlocks human-first applications:

  • Games & leaderboards — verified PvP and fair rewards
  • Earn & microtasks — pay real humans for real work
  • Human-only marketplaces & social commerce
  • DeFi with Sybil resistance — fair incentives and pools
Referral & Rewards

InterLink Wallet couples with a referral system: verified users who bring new verified participants earn $ITLG incentives, fostering organic, human-first growth.

Security & Recovery

Security principles focus on human-friendly, resilient recovery without sacrificing decentralization:

  • Device-based private key custody (non-custodial)
  • Social/recovery options tied to verified human connections
  • Optional hardware-wallet support for advanced users

Note: Recovery mechanisms retain privacy and are designed to prevent centralized access or single points of failure.
