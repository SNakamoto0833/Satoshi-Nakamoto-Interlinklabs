$ITLG & $ITL — Tokenomics & Distribution

Allocation, burning mechanism, and governance — how InterLink tokens power the Human Network.

$ITLG — InterLink Genesis Token

Function: utility & governance token earned by verified humans. Minted through human verification, activity, and referrals.

Utility

  • DAO voting power
  • Ecosystem incentives
  • Launchpad & whitelist privileges
  • Mini-app payments

Burning Mechanism

A percentage of $ITLG used for fees and payments is burned on-chain to maintain scarcity as utility grows. Burn rates will be governed by DAO proposals.

$ITL — InterLink Token (Human Currency)

$ITL is intended as a widely distributed currency used for peer-to-peer payments among verified users and special ecosystem programs. It functions as an accessible medium for everyday on-chain activity tied to real humans.

Allocation principles (illustrative)

  • Human node distribution & rewards
  • Ecosystem & developer grants
  • DAO treasury & strategic reserves
  • Ambassador & community programs

Supply Governance & DAO Oversight

Token supply, burn parameters, and future changes are subject to DAO votes. Once $ITLG reaches predefined maxima, the DAO decides whether to keep fixed supply or strategically expand to welcome more Human Nodes.
